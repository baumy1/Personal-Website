Films

I have been making films since a young age! I first started with a cheap webcam making 3 second stop motions but now am creating short films for school and for fun using Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro, a Parrot Bebop Drone, a GoPro Hero+ LCD and a Lumix DC Vario.

Below are some of my previous projects!

Forward

Forward is a short film that Lachlan Demasi and I made in 2016 for the 15 Words Film Festival. The 15 Words Film Festival runs every year with the limitation of 15 spoken words or less in the film.

Take 3 For The Sea

Take 3 For The Sea is an organisation that is encouraging people around the world to pick up 3 pieces of rubbish when you're at the beach or whenever you can! To help spread the word, my school's sustainability team held a film competition which involved filming yourself picking up 3 pieces of rubbish for the chance to win a GoPro camera. I won the competition with the film below!

Year 7 Camp With a GoPro

For my 2016 school camp, I brought a GoPro camera to film our activities!
